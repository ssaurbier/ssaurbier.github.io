NLP Classification of Similar Subreddits


Identifying differences between /r/ethereum and /r/bitcoin

API (pushshift) used to pull data from Reddit Subreddits and create an NLP model to predict the origin of a Subreddit based on title text. Sentiment analysis included. Bitcoin and ethereum may seem similar to non-crypto users, and while they started with similar goals, they have substantively diverged in archictecture, application, usage, and ultimately community. Ethereum is in many ways a direct response to the capture and ossification of bitcoin core, and the ecosystem faced a crisis over the blocksize (monetary velocity) limit, culminating in 2016. Blockstream coopted bitcoin core, forced out key devs (notably gavin andresen), and purged all communities that did not conform to the new vision, with the help of an anonymous moderator that controlled all major social platforms (Cobra-bitcoin). I parse the text, perform feature engineering, remove key identifiers, and classify text with a variety of models. Surprisingly, even with custom stop words for easily-classifiable information, users of the subreddits are easily identifiable, and discuss different topics. However, sentiment and tone between the two subreddits are similar.
